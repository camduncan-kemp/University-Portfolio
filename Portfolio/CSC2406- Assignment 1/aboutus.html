About Us

Black Rose Publishing was established in 1994 in the town of Gladstone, Queensland. Originally, we were solely focused on publishing books of German comedy, however, since no such books have ever been written, we decided to switch to the wholely more sensible genre of Noir Crime.


Our Prime Directive is to find the best up and coming Noir Crime authors and bring their genius to the rest of the world.
