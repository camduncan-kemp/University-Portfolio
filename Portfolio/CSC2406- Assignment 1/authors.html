The Authors of Black Rose Publishing

At Black Rose Publishing, we publish the masterpieces of some of the world's greatest authors.

Our most prominent writers include the following literary geniuses:

Stephen Fry

Stephen Fry is best known for his stand-alone novels such as The Liar and The Hippopotamus. His writing style is renowned for the use of arcane language and extensive vocabulary in particular.

Benjamin 'Yahtzee' Croshaw

Benjamin Croshaw is best known throughout the online community as Yahtzee the creator of the hit web-series Zero Punctuation but he has also made quite the name for himself as a novellist. His novels are all stand-alone stories that take place in their own unique version of the world, and his writing style is an ecclectic mix of sarcasm, wit and very dark humour/themes.

Douglas Adams

DON'T PANIC

Douglas Adams is an author who needs very little introduction, there are few people in the English speaking world who hasn't at least heard of the much-loved Hitchhiker's Guide To The Galaxy series. Known for his extremely unique blend of dry and sarcastic humour mixed in with dark themes make for a style that is instantly recognisable to readers.
